PROCEDURE ORIENTED PROGRAMMING:-
-------------------------------

** MAIN TASK WILL CLASSIFIED INTO SUBTASK THOSE TASKS REPRESENTED IN THE FORM OF FUNCTIONS.

** TOP-TO-BOTTOM APPROACH.

** NO ACCESS MODIFIERS IN POP(NO DATA HIDING)

** NO METHOD OVERLOADING 

OBJECT ORIENTED PROGRAMMING:-
------------------------------

** MAIN TASK WILL CLASSIFIED INTO SUBTASK THOSE TASKS REPRESENTED IN THE FORM OF OBJECTS.

** BOTTOM-TO-TOP APPROACH.

**ACCESS MODIFIERS IN OOPS.(DATA HIDING)

**METHOD OVERLOADING IS THERE.
